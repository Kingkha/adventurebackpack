Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What’s the best way to get to Chumphon for island hopping?

A: Chumphon is easily accessible by overnight train, bus, or direct flight from Bangkok. The train offers a scenic and comfortable journey, while budget airlines provide quick routes to Chumphon Airport, which connects to the city and piers via shuttle.

Q: Are there direct ferries from Chumphon to popular islands like Koh Tao?

A: Yes, Chumphon serves as a major gateway to Koh Tao, Koh Phangan, and Koh Samui. Several ferry companies operate daily services from Thung Makham Noi Pier and Mattapon Pier, offering both slow and high-speed options.
