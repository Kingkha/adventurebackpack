Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need prior kayaking experience for Kamakura tours?

A: No, many sea kayaking tours in Kamakura are designed for beginners, offering stable kayaks and thorough instruction. Guides provide all necessary safety briefings and equipment, ensuring a comfortable experience for first-timers.
