Q: What’s the best time of year for hiking near Kumamoto?

A: Spring (March to May) and Autumn (September to November) are generally considered the best times for hiking in Kumamoto. The weather is milder, and the landscapes are beautiful with cherry blossoms or autumn foliage. Summer can be hot and humid, while winter can bring snow to higher elevations, requiring different gear.
