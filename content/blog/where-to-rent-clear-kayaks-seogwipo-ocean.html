Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the best time of year to go clear kayaking in Seogwipo?

A: The ideal time for clear kayaking in Seogwipo is usually during late spring (May-June) and early autumn (September-October) when the weather is mild, skies are clear, and the ocean is generally calm. Summer is also popular, but can be very hot and crowded. Always check local forecasts before you head out.

Q: Do I need prior kayaking experience to rent a clear kayak?

A: No, prior kayaking experience is generally not required. Most operators provide basic instructions and safety briefings before you set off. Clear kayaks are stable and relatively easy to maneuver, making them suitable for beginners and families looking for a unique water activity.
