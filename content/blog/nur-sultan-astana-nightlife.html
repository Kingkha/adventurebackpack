Nur-Sultan (Astana) Nightlife

As the sun sets over the futuristic skyline of Nur-Sultan (formerly known as Astana), the city transforms into a vibrant playground where nightlife pulses with energy. From stylish lounges to lively nightclubs, this capital city of Kazakhstan is teeming with options that cater to both locals and international visitors looking to experience its unique offerings. Dive into the heart of the scene with our guide to the top 10 must-visit nightlife spots, where every night promises a new adventure and unforgettable experiences.

If you’re on the lookout for thrilling activities during your visit, check out the curated experiences that showcase the best of what the city has to offer, from breathtaking landmarks to cultural reflectors. For the adventurers among you, our guide to adventures highlights outdoor pursuits and excursions that truly embody Kazakhstan’s rich natural landscape. Meanwhile, immerse yourself in the local culture with insights from our cultural guide that reveals the traditions and history that shape this modern metropolis.

Whether you’re searching for a prominent landmark to visit or want to know what events are lighting up the city, our resources on landmarks and events provide essential information for an authentic Nur-Sultan experience. From breathtaking architectural highlights to lively festivities, the city is alive with excitement. Prepare for a thrilling night out filled with exceptional sights, sounds, and flavors as we explore the nightlife that makes Nur-Sultan a destination not to be missed!

1. Experience the Rooftop Views at Sky Lounge

Nights in Nur-Sultan (Astana) are best experienced from the Sky Lounge, where stunning panoramic views of the city are complemented by a stylish ambiance. As you sip on expertly crafted cocktails, you can revel in the breathtaking skyline that sparkles under the stars. Notably, the Sky Lounge offers a variety of seating options, allowing you to choose between an intimate spot or a lively area filled with fellow revelers.

Moreover, their happy hour specials make it an attractive destination for both locals and tourists alike. If you’re lucky, you might catch live DJ sets that elevate the atmosphere even further. Therefore, make sure to arrive early to secure the best seats!

2. Discover Local Flavors at The Kazakh Bar

The Kazakh Bar is a must-visit for those seeking to immerse themselves in authentic local culture while enjoying delightful drinks. Nestled in the heart of Nur-Sultan, this bar specializes in traditional Kazakh beverages, giving you a unique taste of the region. The welcoming, rustic decor adds to the charm, making it a perfect place to unwind after a long day of exploration.

Transitioning from the hustle and bustle outside, you can savor local snacks while enjoying a live band or a cozy chat with friends. Furthermore, the staff is notably friendly and ready to recommend drinks that pair well with your meal. Thus, experiencing the Kazakh Bar is not just about drinks; it’s about genuine cultural connection.

3. Enjoy Live Music at 7th Floor Club

If live music is your scene, then the 7th Floor Club is the place to be in Nur-Sultan. Known for hosting a variety of local and international artists, this vibrant spot offers a fantastic ambiance that keeps the energy high throughout the night. From jazz to modern pop, there’s something for every music lover.

In addition to outstanding performances, the club serves a fine selection of drinks and delectable dishes. You’ll find that the friendly crowd creates an inviting vibe, encouraging dancing and mingling. Notably, the layout of the club ensures great sightlines, so you won’t miss any of the action on stage. Hence, it is advisable to check their schedule for upcoming performances!

4. Dance the Night Away at The Villa Night Club

If you’re looking to experience vibrant nightlife in Nur-Sultan, The Villa Night Club is the perfect place to dance the night away. This popular venue not only offers great music but also features a chic, modern decor that adds to its allure.

The club regularly hosts well-known DJs who spin an exciting mix of genres, ensuring that every night is memorable. Moreover, the atmosphere is electric; you’ll find both locals and tourists coming together to enjoy the lively ambiance. It’s an ideal spot to let loose and meet new friends.

On weekends, be sure to arrive early to secure a good spot, as the club tends to fill up quickly. Additionally, don’t forget to check their social media for any special events or guest DJ performances that might enhance your Nur-Sultan (Astana) nightlife experience!

5. Relax with Craft Cocktails at Bar 12:27

Located in the heart of the city, Bar 12:27 is a hidden gem for those seeking to unwind during their adventures in Nur-Sultan. This trendy bar is renowned for its innovative craft cocktails, which are expertly mixed by talented bartenders who are passionate about their craft.

As you sip on a delicious cocktail, you can enjoy the cozy ambiance that makes this location a perfect retreat from the bustling nightlife outside. Furthermore, Bar 12:27 often features live music performances, enhancing the relaxed vibe.

When visiting, be sure to try their signature cocktail, which showcases local flavors. It’s a fantastic way to immerse yourself in the culture while savoring a drink. Overall, this bar is a great contrast to the energetic atmosphere found in other venues, making it a must-see in your exploration of Nur-Sultan’s nightlife.

6. Explore the Cultural Vibes at The Bolashak Art Center

The Bolashak Art Center is an exceptional space in Nur-Sultan that combines culture and nightlife, making it a great destination for art lovers and socialites alike. This venue showcases a variety of exhibitions from local and international artists, creating a dynamic environment filled with creativity.

One of the highlights of visiting The Bolashak is the opportunity to participate in art workshops or enjoy cultural events that are often held here. In addition, the center frequently hosts live performances that include music, theater, and dance.

While you immerse yourself in the arts, you can also enjoy refreshments at the on-site café. It’s a great place to relax and reflect on your experience. Therefore, if you’re interested in blending cultural vibes with your nightlife in Nur-Sultan, this art center is an essential stop on your list.

7. Unwind at the Cozy Atmosphere of The Tea House

If you’re looking for a tranquil escape amidst the bustling Nur-Sultan (Astana) nightlife, The Tea House is the perfect retreat. Nestled in one of the city’s quieter neighborhoods, this establishment boasts a welcoming ambiance that encourages relaxation and conversation. The décor is thoughtfully designed with traditional Kazakh elements, making it a delightful spot to unwind.

While enjoying a variety of aromatic teas, you can also indulge in light snacks and desserts. Importantly, the friendly staff is always ready to recommend their best brews, so don’t hesitate to ask for suggestions!

Pro Tip: Visit in the evening to experience the soothing atmosphere, perfect for small gatherings with friends or a peaceful solo retreat.

8. Attend an Event at The Palace of Independence

The Palace of Independence is not only a stunning architectural landmark but also a hub for various cultural and political events. When evening falls, this venue often hosts concerts, exhibitions, and performances that highlight the rich Kazakh culture. Attending an event here can be a remarkable way to experience Nur-Sultan’s vibrant nightlife.

Furthermore, it is advisable to check the schedule ahead of your visit. Many events provide a unique glimpse into local traditions and art, ensuring a memorable experience.

9. Savor International Cuisine at Daredzhani Restaurant

For those seeking to explore the culinary side of Nur-Sultan (Astana) nightlife, Daredzhani Restaurant serves a delightful range of international dishes. The elegant setting, combined with an extensive menu, makes it an ideal choice for both casual diners and those celebrating a special occasion.

As you dine, be sure to try their signature dishes, which beautifully blend local ingredients with international flair. With a well-curated wine list, you can pair your meal perfectly to elevate your dining experience. Remember, reservations are recommended on weekends due to its rising popularity!

10. Get a Taste of Nightlife at Astana Mall

When you think of Nur-Sultan (Astana) nightlife, Astana Mall is a must-visit spot. This vibrant venue not only features a shopping experience but also an exciting nightlife scene! As you step into the mall, you’ll be greeted by an array of entertainment options that cater to all tastes.

First, Astana Mall is home to a variety of bars and lounges. For instance, you can find trendy spots offering signature cocktails designed to please your palate. Moreover, the lively ambiance will set the perfect mood for a night out. In addition to drinks, this mall often hosts live performances that create a festive atmosphere.

Furthermore, you can enjoy delicious dining options that serve both local and international cuisine. Whether you are looking for a casual bite or a full dining experience, Astana Mall has it all. Thus, it serves as a great starting point for diving into the rich nightlife of Nur-Sultan.
