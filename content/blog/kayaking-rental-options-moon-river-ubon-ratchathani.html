Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How much does it cost to rent a kayak on the Moon River?

A: Kayak rentals on the Moon River typically range from 100-300 baht per hour or 500-800 baht for a full day. Prices vary based on the vendor, kayak type (single or double), and rental duration. Life vests and paddles are usually included.

Q: Do I need prior experience to kayak on the Moon River?

A: No, prior experience is generally not required. The Moon River’s waters are usually calm and suitable for beginners, especially in the common rental areas. Most rental places offer stable sit-on-top kayaks perfect for first-timers.

Q: What should I bring for a kayaking trip on the Moon River?

A: Bring sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, plenty of water, a waterproof bag for electronics, and comfortable quick-drying clothes. It’s also wise to have a change of clothes for after your paddle. Don’t forget your camera for stunning photos!
