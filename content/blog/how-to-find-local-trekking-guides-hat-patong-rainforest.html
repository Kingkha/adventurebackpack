Booking & Budgeting Your Rainforest Trek

Understanding the economics of a guided trek is vital. Prices for a Hat Patong rainforest trekking guide can vary based on duration, group size, and the guide’s experience. Typically, you can expect to pay anywhere from 1,500 to 3,000 THB per day for a private guide, though group tours will be less per person. Always clarify what’s included in the price – transport, entry fees, meals, and water. Don’t be afraid to politely negotiate, especially if you’re booking off-season or as part of a larger group.
