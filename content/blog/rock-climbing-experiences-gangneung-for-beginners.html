Q: Is rock climbing dangerous for beginners?

A: While rock climbing inherently involves risks, guided beginner experiences with certified instructors significantly mitigate danger. You’ll learn safety protocols, use proper gear, and climb on routes designed for your skill level, making it a very safe and enjoyable activity.

Q: Do I need special gear for my first time?

A: No, most reputable climbing schools and guided tours in Gangneung provide all the necessary equipment, including harnesses, helmets, climbing shoes, and ropes. You just need to bring comfortable, athletic clothing and a good attitude!

Q: What’s the best time of year to climb in Gangneung?

A: Spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) offer the most pleasant weather for outdoor rock climbing in Gangneung, with mild temperatures and clear skies. Summer can be hot and humid, while winter brings cold temperatures, making indoor climbing or other activities more suitable.
