Q: Are there any entrance fees for Mae Sot’s hidden waterfalls?

A: Larger national park waterfalls, like Pha Charoen, usually have a small entrance fee for foreigners. Smaller, more hidden spots might not, but be prepared for potential local donations or guide fees if you hire one.

Q: Is it safe to swim in all waterfalls near Mae Sot?

A: While generally safe, always exercise caution. Check water depth, look for strong currents, and be mindful of slippery rocks, especially after heavy rains. Local advice is invaluable for ensuring a safe swim.
