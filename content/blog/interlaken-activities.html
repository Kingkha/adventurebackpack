Interlaken Activities

Interlaken is a stunning Swiss town sitting between two pristine lakes and surrounded by breathtaking mountains, making it the ultimate destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Known for its exhilarating Interlaken activities, this charming town offers everything from heart-pounding adventures to peaceful nature explorations. In this article, we will guide you through the top 10 Interlaken activities you shouldn’t miss, catering to all types of travelers—from adrenaline junkies to those seeking tranquility in nature’s beauty.

1. Skydiving Over the Swiss Alps: A Thrilling Adventure

If you’re looking for unforgettable Interlaken activities, skydiving over the Swiss Alps is at the top of the list. Imagine jumping from a plane at 14,000 feet, surrounded by breathtaking vistas. As you free-fall, the stunning landscapes of snow-capped peaks and shimmering lakes unfold beneath you.

This exhilarating adventure is not just about the thrill; it’s also about the scenic views. After the rush of free-falling, your parachute opens, allowing you to glide peacefully as you take in the majestic beauty of the Swiss countryside. Furthermore, the trained guides ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

Pro Tip: Book your skydiving adventure in advance to secure the best slots and enjoy the most stunning weather!

2. Hiking to Harder Kulm: Stunning Views Await

Hiking to Harder Kulm is another must-do among Interlaken activities. This popular trail leads hikers to a viewpoint that offers spectacular panoramas of Interlaken and the surrounding mountains. Essentially, the hike is moderate, making it accessible for most fitness levels.

As you trek along, you’ll encounter lush forests, beautiful alpine meadows, and panoramic views of the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau peaks. Once you reach the top, relax at the restaurant and indulge in some local Swiss delicacies.

Tip: Sunrise hikes provide a magical experience, as the first light casts a golden glow over the landscape.

3. Paragliding Above Interlaken: Soar Like a Bird

For those seeking an aerial perspective, paragliding above Interlaken is undoubtedly one of the most thrilling Interlaken activities. Take off from Beatenberg and soar high above the picturesque landscape, feeling the breeze as you glide effortlessly.

This experience provides a unique viewpoint of Interlaken’s lakes and mountains. Furthermore, tandem flights with expert guides ensure that even beginners can enjoy this exhilarating adventure. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a first-time flyer, paragliding is a fantastic way to create unforgettable memories.

Advice: Try to go paragliding during the late afternoon for the best thermals and spectacular sunset views!

4. Lake Brienz Boat Tours: Explore the Turquoise Waters

Embarking on a Lake Brienz boat tour is a must-do when visiting Interlaken. This breathtaking lake, known for its stunning turquoise color, offers a serene escape from the hustle and bustle. Whether you choose a guided tour or a private boat rental, the experience is sure to be unforgettable.

As you glide across the water, you’ll be surrounded by majestic mountain peaks. Transitioning from one picturesque viewpoint to another, you may even catch glimpses of quaint villages nestled along the shoreline. Each stop presents a unique opportunity to explore hidden gems and take in the beauty of the Swiss Alps.

Additionally, don’t forget to bring your camera. The views are simply spectacular! Overall, a boat tour on Lake Brienz is a relaxing way to immerse yourself in the natural beauty of this stunning region.

5. Canyoning in Interlaken: An Adrenaline Rush Through Nature

For those seeking adventure, canyoning in Interlaken is the perfect choice. This thrilling activity allows you to descend through spectacular gorges and glacial rivers, experiencing Switzerland’s raw beauty firsthand. You can navigate through natural slides, waterfalls, and pools, truly embracing the spirit of adventure.

Moreover, experienced guides ensure your safety while providing expert tips on tackling various obstacles. Notably, this activity offers a blend of excitement and teamwork, making it ideal for friends or families. After a day of excitement, you’ll undoubtedly leave with unforgettable memories and a renewed appreciation for nature.

“Canyoning is not just an activity; it’s an immersive experience that connects you with the stunning landscapes of Interlaken.”

6. Skiing and Snowboarding: Winter Wonderland Excitement

When winter blankets Interlaken in snow, skiing and snowboarding opportunities abound. The nearby slopes offer a variety of trails catering to all skill levels, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the winter wonderland. Not only do you get to ski on pristine snow, but you also experience breathtaking alpine scenery.

The best part is the accessibility of ski passes, making it easy to hop between different resorts. Furthermore, many local instructors provide lessons for beginners, guaranteeing a fun and safe experience. As you glide down the slopes, you can soak in the panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, which elevate the entire experience.

In conclusion, whether you’re a seasoned skier or catching your first snowflakes, skiing and snowboarding in Interlaken is an exhilarating way to embrace the magic of winter.

7. Bungee Jumping from the Stockhorn: Dare to Take the Leap

For thrill-seekers, bungee jumping from the Stockhorn offers an experience like no other. This activity allows you to leap off a platform, suspended 134 meters above the ground, with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys below. As you stand on the edge, your heart races with anticipation, and when you finally take the plunge, the adrenaline rush is truly unparalleled.

Once you jump, you’ll experience a freefall that lasts several seconds before the elastic cord pulls you back up. Many jumpers describe it as a life-changing experience, so don’t miss the chance to face your fears and conquer the jump. Remember, safety is prioritized, with professional instructors ensuring that all equipment is up to standard.

8. Visit the Jungfraujoch: Top of Europe Experience

Another must-visit destination in the Interlaken region is the incredible Jungfraujoch, known as the “Top of Europe.” Accessible via a scenic train ride, this UNESCO World Heritage site boasts majestic views of snow-capped peaks. As you arrive at the highest railway station in Europe, you will be greeted by a breathtaking panorama of the Bernese Alps.

While visiting Jungfraujoch, make sure to explore the stunning Ice Palace filled with intricate ice sculptures and walk onto the Aletsch Glacier for a memorable experience. Also, don’t forget to grab a bite at the restaurant, which offers Swiss specialties alongside incredible mountainous views. As you immerse yourself in this beautiful landscape, you’ll understand why it is a highlight of Interlaken activities.

9. Cycling the Interlaken Trails: Scenic Rides for All Levels

Cycling in Interlaken is the perfect way to explore the beauty of this stunning region while enjoying some outdoor exercise. The trails here cater to all skill levels, so both beginners and experienced cyclists can find a pathway that suits their needs. One popular route takes you around Lake Thun, where you can enjoy picturesque views of the water and surrounding mountains.

Additionally, rental services are available throughout the town, making it easy to grab a bike and hit the trails. You can even join a guided tour that ensures you don’t miss any of the area’s hidden gems. Whether you prefer leisurely rides or challenging terrains, biking in Interlaken is a fantastic way to experience nature at your own pace.

10. Relaxing at the Lake Thun Shore: A Peaceful Escape

Nestled just a short distance from Interlaken, Lake Thun offers a perfect retreat for those seeking relaxation. The tranquil shorelines are ideal for unwinding and enjoying the serene beauty of the Swiss landscape. As you stroll along the lake, the scenic views of the surrounding mountains and lush greenery will captivate your senses.

Moreover, visitors can choose to engage in various leisurely activities. For instance, picnicking on the grassy areas is a popular choice. Alternatively, you could simply sit on a bench and soak in the breathtaking views. The aroma of the fresh air, combined with the sound of gentle waves, creates an atmosphere perfect for relaxation.

Pro Tip: Early morning visits offer a chance to see the sun rise over the lake, illuminating the water with golden hues.

If you’re more adventurous, consider renting a kayak or paddleboard. This way, you can explore the turquoise waters while enjoying a unique perspective of the enchanting shores. In conclusion, relaxing at Lake Thun is one of the best Interlaken activities that shouldn’t be missed.

Interlaken truly offers a diverse array of activities that cater to every type of adventurer. From adrenaline-pumping skydiving and paragliding to serene boat tours and lakeside relaxation, there is something for everyone in this gorgeous Swiss landscape.
