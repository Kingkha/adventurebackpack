Batumi Events

The Annual Batumi Music Festival is a highlight in the city’s calendar, drawing music lovers from all around. If you’re considering attending, be sure to check the festival’s schedule in advance to catch your favorite artists. Overall, this festival is a wonderful celebration of talent, making it one of the top Batumi Events to experience.

4. Celebrating Georgian Wine: The Batumi Wine Fest Experience

The Batumi Wine Festival is a vibrant celebration of Georgia’s renowned winemaking heritage. This annual event draws wine enthusiasts from all around the world, offering them a chance to savor a wide variety of local wines. Additionally, festival-goers can enjoy fascinating workshops on wine pairing, bottling, and vineyard tours.

Attending the festival not only allows you to taste exquisite wines but also to engage with local winemakers. The warm hospitality and knowledge of the vendors enhance the overall experience, making it memorable. Moreover, various local dishes are paired with the wines, showcasing Georgian culinary traditions.

Pro Tip: Be sure to try the chacha, a traditional grape spirit that is often a highlight at the festival!

Food lovers rejoice at the Batumi Street Food Festival, where the city’s vibrant culinary scene comes alive. This event not only showcases a variety of delicious street food but also Highlights the rich flavors of Georgian cuisine. From khachapuri to lobio, there’s something for every palate.

Furthermore, live music and cooking demonstrations create a lively atmosphere that enhances the dining experience. As a bonus, many vendors share their secret recipes, allowing you to recreate these scrumptious dishes at home.
