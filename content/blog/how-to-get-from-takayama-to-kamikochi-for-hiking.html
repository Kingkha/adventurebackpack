Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What’s the best time of year to visit Kamikochi for hiking?

A: Kamikochi is typically open from mid-April to mid-November. The best times for hiking are late spring (May-June) for lush greenery and autumn (October) for spectacular fall foliage. Summers (July-September) are popular but can be rainy.

Q: Can I drive directly into Kamikochi?

A: No, private vehicles are not allowed past the Kama Tunnel. You must park at a designated parking lot outside Kamikochi (e.g., Hirayu Onsen or Akandana Parking Lot) and then take a shuttle bus or taxi into the valley.

Q: How long does the bus journey from Takayama to Kamikochi take?

A: The bus journey, including the transfer at Hirayu Onsen, generally takes about 1.5 to 2 hours, depending on bus schedules and waiting times for the connection. It’s a scenic ride, so enjoy the views!
