Safety Tips

Safety is paramount when traveling, and Patong Beach is generally safe for tourists. However, like any tourist destination, it is important to stay vigilant. Here are a few key safety tips:

  • Beware of Scams: While most locals are friendly, be cautious of those offering overly cheap tours or services. Always opt for reputable companies.
