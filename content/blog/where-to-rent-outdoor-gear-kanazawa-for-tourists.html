Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I rent hiking boots in Kanazawa?

A: While less common than bicycle rentals, some specialized outdoor shops or larger sports retailers in Kanazawa might offer hiking boot rentals. It’s best to call ahead or check their websites. Alternatively, ensure you bring comfortable, broken-in hiking shoes if you plan extensive trail walking.

Q: Is it expensive to rent outdoor gear in Kanazawa?

A: The cost varies depending on the type of gear and rental duration. Bicycle rentals are generally affordable, ranging from ¥500-¥1500 per day. More specialized items like camping equipment would be pricier. Always compare rates and check for multi-day discounts.

Q: What kind of identification is needed for gear rental?

A: Most rental shops in Kanazawa will require a valid form of identification, typically your passport or a Japanese residence card. Some may also ask for a credit card deposit. It’s always a good idea to confirm requirements with the rental provider in advance to avoid any delays.
