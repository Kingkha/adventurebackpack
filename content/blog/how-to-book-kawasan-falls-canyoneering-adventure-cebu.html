Choosing the Right Canyoneering Operator

Selecting a reputable operator is paramount for a safe and enjoyable canyoneering experience. Many local operators bundle packages that include transport from Cebu City, gear rental, guide fees, and even a post-canyoneering meal. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does the Kawasan Falls canyoneering adventure take?

A: The Kawasan Falls canyoneering adventure typically takes about 3 to 4 hours from start to finish, including safety briefings and the trek through the canyons. This duration can vary slightly depending on the group size and speed.

Q: Do I need to know how to swim for canyoneering?

A: While strong swimming skills are not strictly required, basic comfort in water is beneficial. You will be provided with a life vest, and guides are always there to assist. The deepest sections involve floating and short swims.

Q: What’s the best time of year to go canyoneering in Kawasan Falls?

A: The best time to go canyoneering in Kawasan Falls is during Cebu’s dry season, typically from December to May. The water levels are usually ideal, and the weather is sunny, making for perfect conditions for outdoor activities.
