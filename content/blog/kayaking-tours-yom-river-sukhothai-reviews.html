It’s not a turbulent, challenging river; instead, it offers a peaceful, scenic paddle perfect for all skill levels, from beginners to experienced kayakers. Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is kayaking on the Yom River suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, absolutely! The Yom River is generally calm and slow-moving, making it ideal for beginners and families. Most tours provide stable kayaks and expert guides who offer instructions and ensure a safe, enjoyable experience for everyone.

Q: What’s the best time of year for kayaking in Sukhothai?

A: The cooler, dry season from November to February is generally considered the best time for kayaking, offering pleasant temperatures and lower humidity. However, the river is navigable year-round, with the green season (May-October) providing lush scenery, though with higher chances of rain.

Q: What should I bring for a Yom River kayaking tour?

A: Pack light, quick-drying clothing, a hat, sunglasses, and reef-safe sunscreen. A reusable water bottle is essential, and consider a waterproof bag for electronics like your phone or camera. Most tour operators provide life vests and all necessary paddling equipment.
