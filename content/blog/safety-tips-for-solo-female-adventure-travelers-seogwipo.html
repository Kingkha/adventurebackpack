Building a Support Network & Emergency Protocols

Even as a solo traveler, you’re never truly alone if you build a small support network. Familiarize yourself with the location of your country’s embassy or consulate in South Korea and their emergency contact details. While it’s unlikely you’ll need them, knowing is reassuring. Learn a few basic Korean phrases like “help me” (도와주세요 – dowajuseyo) or “emergency” (긴급 상황 – ginggeup sanghwang). These simple phrases can make a big difference if you find yourself in a tight spot.
